IPU Series Overview

MAIN FEATURES

  • 1CH/4CH/8CH analog video PSU products
  • Universal HD analog video (AHD, TVI, CVI, SD)
  • Full OSD operation via on-board UTC (Up to Coax)
  • Adaptive source voltage control for optional voltage levels
  • Power Devices detect and control power source sourcing to minimize damage to generic cameras without Power circuits.
  • Video signal detection for secure control of sourcing power
  • Signal line status LEDs for each channel (Video Connection/Power Sourcing Status)
  • Protection circuits on power line ( Power line protection circuit )
  • Noise filer for power line noise
  • Metal shielding minimizes crosstalk between video channel BNC
  • ESD and Surge Protection Circuits
  • Manual power on/off control for each channel
  • Transmission distance: Up to 400m (5C coaxial cable)

  • DIMENSION
